An experimental macro tracker: Macros For Humans

Macros for humans

I built an app called Macros for Humans and "launched" it on the Play Store. I think my mom downloaded it.

This was my first mobile app and I was pretty proud of it, but the form factor felt wrong. Installing an app and learning the UI just to stay on your diet feels like too much friction.

So I went back to the drawing board and rebuilt Macros for Humans from the ground up as an experimental, SMS-only, post-app nutrition tracker.

No app. No dashboard. No website. Nothing.

Just text what you ate, and let an agent handle the annoying parts of macro tracking.

Stop tapping, start texting

Macros for Humans is an experiment in removing friction from tracking your macros. You just have to report the facts.

Would you like to give it a free try? Sign up today at https://macrosforhumans.com.
